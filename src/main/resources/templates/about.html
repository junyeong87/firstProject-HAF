Hot & Funny

The 2023 Seoul International Rock Festival was
first held back in 1999 when it was known as 'Asian Rock Festival'.
In 2000 it was rebranded as The Seoul International Rock Festival and now,
in its 24th year has become one of Korea's longest running and most dynamic festivals.
The event takes place at SEJONG CENTER FOR THE PERFROMING ARTS (in Gwanghwamun Gate) and
features an eclectic lineup of the best up and coming artists
headliners from Korea and around the world.
The 2023 Seoul International Rock Festival also promotes
artist development through long established exchange
programs with international festivals across asia.
